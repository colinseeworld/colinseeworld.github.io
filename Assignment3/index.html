Travel in Japan

Kiyomizu-dera, formally Otowa-san Kiyomizu-dera, is a Buddhist temple in eastern Kyoto.

Ginkakuji ( Silver Pavilion) is a Zen temple along Kyoto's eastern mountains (Higashiyama).

Mount Fuji (Fujisan, IPA: [ɸɯꜜ(d)ʑisaɴ] (About this soundlisten)), located on the island of Honshū, is the highest mountain in Japan, standing 3,776.24 m (12,389.2 ft).
